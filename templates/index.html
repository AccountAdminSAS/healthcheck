Calculate your health score

A comprehensive health check calculator is a valuable tool designed to help individuals assess various aspects of their health and well-being. By considering factors such as lifestyle habits, nutrition, and physical activity, this tool provides personalised insights and recommendations to improve overall health. Unlike traditional methods that rely on a single metric, like Body Mass Index (BMI), the comprehensive health check calculator offers a more holistic view, ensuring that users receive a thorough analysis tailored to their unique needs.

Whether you're looking to track fitness progress, manage weight, or optimise your daily habits, this calculator serves as a user-friendly guide for taking proactive steps toward a healthier lifestyle.

After receiving your 'health score,' you will gain a clearer understanding of your overall health.
